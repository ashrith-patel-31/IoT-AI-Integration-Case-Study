# IoT & Artificial Intelligence Integration Case Study

## 📖 Overview

This project was completed as part of the **CodeAlpha Internet of Things (IoT) Internship Program**.

The case study explores the integration of **Internet of Things (IoT)** and **Artificial Intelligence (AI)** technologies. It examines how IoT devices collect real-time data from the physical environment and how AI analyzes that data to enable intelligent decision-making, automation, prediction, and optimization.

The study covers architecture, working mechanisms, applications, benefits, challenges, and future opportunities of IoT-AI systems across various industries.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Understand the fundamentals of IoT and AI.
* Study the integration of IoT and Artificial Intelligence.
* Analyze real-world applications of IoT-AI systems.
* Examine benefits and challenges of intelligent systems.
* Explore future developments in IoT and AI technologies.

---

## 🌐 What is IoT?

The Internet of Things (IoT) refers to a network of interconnected physical devices equipped with sensors, software, and communication technologies that collect and exchange data through the internet.

### Examples

* Smart Home Devices
* Wearable Health Monitors
* Smart Agriculture Sensors
* Industrial Monitoring Systems

---

## 🤖 What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that enables machines to learn from data, identify patterns, make decisions, and solve problems with minimal human intervention.

### Examples

* Machine Learning Systems
* Virtual Assistants
* Recommendation Systems
* Predictive Analytics

## ⚙ Working Mechanism

1. Data is collected through IoT sensors.
2. Information is transmitted through communication networks.
3. Data is stored in cloud or edge platforms.
4. AI algorithms analyze collected information.
5. Intelligent decisions are generated.
6. Automated actions or notifications are triggered.
